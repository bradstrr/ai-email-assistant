What does the AI assistant do?

Our assistant reads your Gmail inbox, understands the context of messages, and generates intelligent draft replies you can review, edit, and send.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your email data is only accessed with your permission and is not stored permanently. Drafts are generated locally in your session.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation required. Our tool works entirely in your browser after Google login authorization.

Can I customize the replies?

Absolutely. You can edit every draft before sending. You can also add your own website and email signature for more personalized responses.
